# Different Types of Stress

Because the phonology section of the book is talking about multiple kinds of stress, they thought it would be a good idea to use different classifying terms. Of course, this is after we’ve already been reading about stress for a chapter, but whatever.

Prominence

Prominence is defined as the emphasis of words in a sentence - some words are naturally higher pitched, more forceful, etc. This highlights the intended meaning or the subject of interest in the sentence.

Accent

Accent is defined as the emphasis of a syllable in a word that is more or less a fixed attribute in a word. Saying the word in isolation or seeing it in a dictionary will generate this type of stress.

Meta

What does this mean? It means that we are talking about different phenomena when we talk about stress. Syllable stress in a word, and word stress in a sentence.
