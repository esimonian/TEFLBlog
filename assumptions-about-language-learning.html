Assumptions About Language Learning and Teaching?

03 Oct 2016 on Lexical, Teaching

Are apparently a huge deal and in one of the sections of the DELTA. Unfortunately, there are NONE in the assigned reading for the task I’m working on. Do I draw these assumptions from other readings in this week? From the course material? From THE FUCKING AIR?

Now I’m looking at old tests (they do release them, neat) and blogs from other people who have taken the DELTA. I’m going to start listing some assumptions.

  1. Personalization
  2. Collaboration/pairwork
  3. Visuals
  4. Learning styles
  5. Language in context
  6. Activating previous knowledge or schemata
  7. Integrated focus on skills
  8. Variety
  9. Textuality
  10. Process modeling

man, I don’t know. I’m going to have to come back to these assumptions, they’re apparently extremely important.
